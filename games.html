Games

A Snapshot Of My Life is a walking simulator about how my life had been affected by covid 19. It's an interactive art gallery demonstrating where my headspace was at during this time of my life.

What Would Have Been is a text-based narrative walking the player through my normal college life of waking up in the morning and going to class. It may be mundane, but it is something that I missed a lot while being stuck in quarantine during the end of my senior year of undergrad.
