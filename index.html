Curiosity Is Everywhere, Especially Underwater. Drawn as part of MerMay, an online prompt/challenge for artists to draw a mermaid every day for the month of May. The prompt that inspired this artwork was “curious” and what could promote more curiosity than ocean life. Even mermaids are fascinated by jellyfish, they move in such peculiar ways. This artwork is definitely one of my favorites maybe because I was experimenting with drawing eyes and hair in different ways than I am used to.
