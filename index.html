Drawn as part of MerMay, an online challenge for artists to draw a mermaid every day for the month of May. I was inspired by the word “curious”. This artwork is definitely one of my favorites maybe because I was experimenting with drawing eyes and hair in different ways than I am used to.

I love these two characters and the story that they are written in even more.

I really wanted to design some mickey ears and decided to place them onto a character to make it more fun.
