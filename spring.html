When The Weather Gets Pleasant

Every single year without fail, the moment the weather outside becomes warmer, the tree’s leaves grow back, and flowers begin to bloom again, that’s when I know that allergy season is back. Spring is such a conflicting time of the year, because I love being outdoors in the warm weather but I hate when I start sneezing and have watery eyes. I made this comic to embrace my struggle that I have every year.
