Welcome To Your Imagination

Filled with a collage of various images that all hold different meanings, this painting was my final project for a painting class that I took while at university. I intended the painting to have a perspective that drew the eyes down the center of the image while also allowing for the exploration of all the curious creatures and objects in the scene.
